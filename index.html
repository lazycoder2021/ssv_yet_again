Our Freight Service

By Land

The premium quality transport we offer ensure customer shipments reach their destination on time. And we take all precautions – like providing a temperature-controlled environment, safe distancing etc., – needed to keep our shipments in perfect condition.

By Air

We work with all major airlines across India to ensure quick transport of our customer consignments. Each and every consignment is followed up by our air freight professionals and our customers are provided with real time status reports.

By Sea

Port to port (including port to door) movement of customer cargo is possible with our sea freight service. From handling heavy paperwork to providing professionals to load cargo onto shipments (stevedoring) to storing cargo until it leaves the shore, we take care of it all.
